Executive Summary

Selecting the right ERP system is one of the most critical technology decisions for any GCC enterprise. This guide provides an objective comparison of 12 leading platforms based on:

  • Feature completeness for GCC business requirements
  • VAT and ZATCA compliance capabilities
  • Total cost of ownership (3-year analysis)
  • Implementation timeline and complexity
  • Local support and partner ecosystem

Vendor Evaluation Scorecard

Use this scorecard to evaluate vendors based on your specific requirements:

Functional Requirements (40 points)

  • Core financials (GL, AP, AR, FA) - /10
  • Inventory and warehouse management - /10
  • Sales and CRM capabilities - /10
  • Industry-specific modules - /10

Compliance & Localization (25 points)

  • UAE VAT compliance (FTA approved) - /10
  • Saudi ZATCA Phase 2 ready - /10
  • Multi-currency & exchange rate handling - /5

Technical Considerations (20 points)

  • Cloud vs on-premise flexibility - /5
  • API availability & integration capabilities - /5
  • Mobile accessibility - /5
  • Data security & backup - /5

Vendor Strength (15 points)

  • Local partner ecosystem in UAE/GCC - /5
  • Implementation track record - /5
  • Support responsiveness - /5

Key Selection Criteria by Industry

Real Estate & Property Management

Must-have features: Unit management, lease tracking, owner statements, facility management, Ejari integration

Recommended: Odoo, Yardi, PropertyMatrix

Trading & Distribution

Must-have features: Multi-warehouse, lot tracking, landed cost, LC management, supplier portals

Recommended: SAP Business One, Oracle NetSuite, Focus 9

Manufacturing

Must-have features: BOM management, work orders, quality control, shop floor integration, MRP

Recommended: SAP S/4HANA, Microsoft Dynamics, Odoo Manufacturing

Professional Services

Must-have features: Project accounting, timesheet management, resource planning, milestone billing

Recommended: Oracle NetSuite, Microsoft Dynamics, Zoho One
